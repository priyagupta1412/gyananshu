About Us

Welcome to Gyananshu: The Scholar Connect!

Gyananshu is an innovative platform designed exclusively for the Department of Computer Science at Banasthali Vidyapith. Our mission is to empower researchers, faculty members, and students, facilitating seamless collaboration, transparent communication, and academic growth.

Mentorship Opportunities:

Gyananshu provides tools for students to connect with faculty mentors, fostering a culture of mentorship and guidance. We believe in the power of mentor-student relationships in shaping the future of computer science.

Conference and Events:

The portal provides information about upcoming conferences,seminars and academic events related to computer science.

Faculty-Student Interaction:

It provides tools for students to connect with faculty mentors, fostering a culture of mentorship and guidance. This interaction can be instrumental in student development and research projects.

Our Story

Gyananshu is not just a platform; it's a community of passionate individuals dedicated to the pursuit of academic excellence. Whether you are a researcher, faculty member, or student, we invite you to join us on this transformative journey. Together, let's shape the future of computer science and make a lasting impact on the world of academia.
